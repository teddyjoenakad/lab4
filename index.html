Here are some facts about me

Sports have always been in my life. They help me relax in times of trouble and have become therapeutic to me. As a kid with lots of energy, I often played a lot of sports such as basketball, soccer, track, and ping pong. As my athletic abilities began to evolve throughout the years, I began to improve in sports like volleyball and rugby. Once I realized how much I enjoyed rugby and soccer I began to focus on those two sports and dedicated my time to them. I ended up being the captain of both school teams and excelling in both. Now I play sports frequently throughout the week as it helps me feel relieved and acts as a free therapy session.
